About me

I am building my frontend career through real projects and steady practice.

I am a final-year Computer Engineering student who enjoys the part of software that people actually touch: layouts, interfaces, content flow, and the small details that make a product feel easier to use.

Background

A technical foundation with a strong UI interest

My degree gives me the engineering side of problem solving, while my projects pull me toward frontend work. I like building websites that are clean, readable, and responsive across devices.

What matters to me

Clarity, consistency, and usability

Good frontend work is not only about color or animation. It is also about readable structure, predictable spacing, accessible interactions, and helping people understand what to do next.

Now

Sharpening frontend fundamentals

Mindset
